# Contract Review Output Contract

Use markdown. Do not use XML tags.

## Required sections

```markdown
# Contract Review: [Document Name]

**Document Type:** [type]
**Your Position:** [position/assumption]
**Counterparty:** [name/role]
**Risk Level:** 🔴 High / 🟡 Medium / 🟢 Low
**Document Status:** Draft / Unsigned / Executed / Unknown

## ⚠️ Pre-Signing Alerts

## Executive Summary

## Key Terms

## Risk Analysis

### 🔴 Critical

### 🟡 Important

### 🟢 Reviewed & Acceptable

## Missing Provisions

## Internal Consistency Issues

## Negotiation Priority

*This review is for informational purposes only. Material terms should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel.*
```

## Risk finding shape

For each material issue include:

- **Clause / location**
- **Quote** where useful
- **Issue**
- **Risk**
- **Market Standard**
- **Negotiability**: High / Medium / Low / None
- **Redline**
- **Fallback**

## Tables

Use tables for key terms, red flags, acceptable categories, missing provisions, and negotiation priority.

## Style rules

- Be scannable and practical.
- Cite section numbers or clause names when available.
- Mark missing/unclear values explicitly.
- Distinguish document text from your recommendation.
