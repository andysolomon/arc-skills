# SaaS Subscription Agreement

This SaaS Subscription Agreement is entered into by Acme Software Inc. (Vendor) and BetaCo LLC (Customer).

## 1. Term
The initial term is twelve (12) months. The agreement automatically renews for additional twelve (12) month periods unless Customer gives notice at least thirty (30) days before renewal.

## 2. Fees
Customer shall pay $____ per month. Vendor may increase fees upon renewal in its sole discretion.

## 3. Data Ownership
Customer owns all Customer Data.

## 4. Suspension
Vendor may suspend the service immediately in its sole discretion if Vendor believes Customer is misusing the service.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Vendor's aggregate liability shall not exceed fees paid by Customer in the three (3) months before the claim.

## 6. Indemnification
Customer shall indemnify Vendor from all claims arising from Customer's use of the service. Vendor has no reciprocal indemnity obligation.

## 7. Termination
Vendor may terminate this Agreement for convenience on thirty (30) days' notice. Customer may terminate only for uncured material breach after thirty (30) days' notice.

## 8. Data Export
Upon termination, Customer may request export of Customer Data if Vendor makes export tools generally available.
