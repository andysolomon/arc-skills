## main
Title: AI Meal Planner for Diabetics
Problem: Diabetic patients struggle to plan meals that balance glucose targets, preferences, and grocery budgets.
Audience: Adults with Type 2 diabetes and caregivers.

## value-ladder
Offer: Free recipe generator, paid weekly meal plan, premium dietitian review.

## why-now
Why now: GLP-1 adoption and continuous glucose monitors are changing food planning behavior.

## proof-signals
Signals: Search growth for diabetic meal planning; Reddit threads asking for budget diabetic recipes.

## market-gap
Gap: Existing apps track carbs but rarely generate budget-aware grocery lists.

## execution-plan
Plan: Build MVP with onboarding, preference capture, meal generation, grocery list, and CGM-friendly exports.

## keywords
Keywords: diabetic meal plan, type 2 diabetes recipes, low glycemic grocery list
